Story line

Join Zuko Kin on a journey, the worlds First Ascended Child, born of love between a God and a human in a world teetering on the brink of chaos. Endowed with the unique ability to manipulate energy stronger than the Gods themselves, Zuko's destiny unfolds as the fear of power shatter his family when they learn of his future. The Gods, fearing what Zuko may become, begin hunting all of the 2,000 Ascended Children that come from his same lineage, leading to a war that engulfs Nzuri. Fleeing to Enlightened City for hopes of a safe haven, the futuristic metropolis, where to humans the ancient gods are nothing but a myth. As Zuko navigates the forces and the new world surrounding him, his journey becomes a quest for restoring his families honor, becoming the strongest being alive and helping his brothers and sisters survive.
